Story

Imagine a world just like our own. In this world one small mistake in a research lab creates a zombie virus. Unfortunately the zombies are very effective at spreading their disease and what is worse is that the virus has an incubation period. Because of this, the virus spreads rapidly across the country and the world. Simply put, there is no stopping the zombies.
